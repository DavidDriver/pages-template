About Me

I've had the chance to work with some pretty great companies over the years. From Technicolor, where I worked with film processing, to Columbia Pictures, Entertainment Partners, and Central Casting, I've worn many hats in the entertainment industry. Whether it was participations, location accounting, casting, or consulting, I learned a lot and met some fantastic people.

Later, I dove into tech, supporting the travel industry at Princess Cruises. As an XML/API technical administrator, I helped manage the POLAR reservations system, ensuring it ran smoothly for thousands of websites and third-party vendors like Expedia, Travelocity, and AAA.

These days, I’m focused on Linux systems, AI, and automation with Python. I'm particularly interested in web-based technologies and rapid deployment solutions using platforms like GitHub, AWS, and Azure.

When I'm not working on tech projects, you might find me exploring my other passions: photography, music, cooking, and more. Check out the navigation bar above to learn more about my consulting services, recipes, creative projects, and more.
